# Buckwheat Pancakes

Mix in bowl:

- 1 cup / 170 grams buckwheat flour
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- pinch salt

Combine in same bowl:

- 1/2 cup soy milk
- 1/2 tbs oil
- 1 banana mashed

Fry in anti-adhesive pan for ~3 minutes each side.
