# Glazed Butternut

Cook in pan with lid for ~5 mins or until tender:

- a bit of oil
- 1 butternut squash diced
- 1/2 yellow onion diced
- apple cider
- pinch of sugar
- salt/pepper

Remove lid and keep cooking until evaporated and starts browing.

Serve with:

- parsley chopped
