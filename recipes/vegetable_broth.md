# Vegetable Broth

Place in baking sheets lined with parchment paper:

- vegetable scraps

Bake at 150 F (dehydration) for ~6 hrs.

Bake at 350 F for 30 mins.

Add to pot of cold water and cook on low for ~6 hrs.
