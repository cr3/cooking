# Cucumber Gazpacho

- 1 English cucumber or 2 Lebanese cucumbers
- 1 tomatillo
- 1 green onion
- 1 clove garlic
- handful of cilantro
- pinch of salt
- grind of pepper

Blend and pour into bowls.

- creme fraiche
- lime zest

Combine and serve as a quenelle.

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lime juice

Drizzle on top.
